⫻context/meta:summary/0
Framework: Dopamine Design Canvas
Purpose: Architect digital collaboration spaces
Goal: Amplify motivation, connection, innovation via reward system

⫻list/table:triggers/main
| Trigger | Description | Mechanism | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gemeinsame Zielerreichung** | Celebrate micro-wins and milestones to trigger immediate dopamine hits. | Activates reward anticipation (mesolimbic pathway). | Shared dashboard with visual cues (confetti) for completed tasks. |
| **Soziale Zugehörigkeit** | Foster belonging through shared contributions and mutual recognition. | Social validation stimulates oxytocin and dopamine. | Team channel for kudos and emojis. |
| **Neugier & Exploration** | Encourage experimentation and discovery of new ideas. | Novelty-seeking activates curiosity circuits. | Digital sandbox for prototyping wild ideas. |
| **Selbstwirksamkeit** | Empower individuals to see their impact within the collective. | Perceived control enhances motivation. | Contributor badges highlighting individual inputs. |
| **Reflexion & Wachstum** | Make progress visible through reflection and documentation. | Retrospective pride reinforces long-term dopamine cycles. | Shared journal logging achievements and lessons. |
| **Unmittelbarkeit & Feedback-Resonanz** | Provide instant feedback to validate contributions in real-time. | Immediate loops ping the nucleus accumbens. | Live comments/reactions during collaborative sessions. |
| **Gamifikation & Epische Fortschrittsbögen** | Frame collaboration as a narrative quest with clear milestones. | Anticipatory dopamine triggered by "quest" progress. | Project board with "quest levels" and achievement badges. |
| **Synchronisation & Flow-Zustand** | Enable real-time co-creation to induce collective flow. | Flow inhibits default mode network, sustaining focus. | Synchronized Miro board for real-time co-design. |
| **Identitätsbildung & Sinnstiftung** | Connect contributions to a larger, meaningful purpose. | Dopamine signals significance via purpose. | Mission statement co-created and displayed prominently. |
| **Metakognition & Dopamin-Retrospektive** | Reflect on collective progress to amplify pride. | Prefrontal cortex feedback loops enhance reward. | Monthly "highlight reel" session reviewing team wins. |

⫻list/list:elements/main
- **Shared Digital Journal**: Reflexion, Selbstwirksamkeit, Soziale Zugehörigkeit (Notion/custom app)
- **Real-Time Collaboration Tools**: Synchronisation, Unmittelbarkeit, Neugier (Figma, Miro)
- **Gamified Dashboards**: Gamifikation, Gemeinsame Zielerreichung (Trello/Asana)
- **Feedback Loops**: Unmittelbarkeit, Soziale Zugehörigkeit (Slack emojis)
- **Purpose-Driven Framing**: Identitätsbildung, Sinnstiftung (Team Charter)
