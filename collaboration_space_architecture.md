⫻structure/channel:layout/main

    - *Purpose*: Alignment & Identity (North Star)
    - *Trigger*: **Identitätsbildung & Sinnstiftung** (Serotonin/Dopamine)
    - *Visual Theme*: "Command Center" – High contrast, clean typography, hero image of the Mission Statement.
    - *Pinned Content*: The Team Manifesto & Live "Impact Metric" Counter.
- **Channel 02: The Lab**
    - *Purpose*: Curiosity, Prototyping, "What if?"
    - *Trigger*: **Neugier & Exploration** (Dopamine - Novelty)
    - *Visual Theme*: "Workshop" – Blueprint backgrounds, mono-spaced fonts, sketches.
    - *Pinned Content*: "Experiment of the Week" prompt & Link to the "Sandbox" environment.
- **Channel 03: Quest Board**
    - *Purpose*: Progress Tracking & Milestones
    - *Trigger*: **Gamifikation & Gemeinsame Zielerreichung** (Dopamine - Anticipation)
    - *Visual Theme*: "Arcade" – Progress bars, badges, vibrant "Level Up" animations.
    - *Pinned Content*: The "Quest Log" (Current Sprint Goals visualized as monsters/bosses).
- **Channel 04: Watercooler Wins**
    - *Purpose*: Belonging & Recognition
    - *Trigger*: **Soziale Zugehörigkeit** (Oxytocin)
    - *Visual Theme*: "Lounge" – Warm colors (orange/sunset), round avatars, soft edges.
    - *Pinned Content*: "Kudos Wall" & "Meet the Team" spotlight.
- **Channel 05: The Archives**
    - *Purpose*: Reflection & Knowledge
    - *Trigger*: **Reflexion & Wachstum** (Serotonin - Pride)
    - *Visual Theme*: "Library" – Muted greens, serif fonts, quiet layout.
    - *Pinned Content*: "Book of Wisdom" (Wiki link) & "Retrospective Highlights".

⫻process/ritual:flow/main
- **Ritual 01: The Daily Quest Log (Standup)**
    - *Timing*: 09:30 AM
    - *Trigger*: **Selbstwirksamkeit**
    - *Agenda*:
        1. "What monster (task) am I slaying today?" (Reframes work as challenge).
        2. "Who is my support mage?" (Identifies blockers/collaboration).
    - *Outcome*: Clear intent + social commitment.
- **Ritual 02: Spark Session (Deep Work)**
    - *Timing*: Ad-hoc / 2pm Daily
    - *Trigger*: **Synchronisation & Flow-Zustand**
    - *Agenda*:
        1. 5m: State goal in chat.
        2. 25m: Silent co-working (Music bot playing lo-fi).
        3. 5m: "Show & Tell" – rapid fire demo of progress (Immediate feedback).
- **Ritual 03: Level Up Retro (Weekly)**
    - *Timing*: Friday 4:00 PM
    - *Trigger*: **Metakognition & Dopamin-Retrospektive**
    - *Agenda*:
        1. "Loot Drop": Review completed quests (Wins).
        2. "Experience Points": share one thing learned (Growth).
        3. "Healer's Tent": Thank a teammate (Oxytocin).

⫻logic/bot:interactions/main
- **Bot Name**: **DopaBot**
- **Function**: Automated neuro-stimulator.
- **Behaviors**:
    - *The First Win*: Detects the first completed task of the day -> posts a "First Blood!" GIF.
    - *Random Coffee*: Pairs 2 people weekly for a 15 min chat -> Prompt: "Share a non-work hobby" (Oxytocin).
    - *Flow Guard*: If a user is in "Spark Mode", auto-replies to DMs with "Currently slaying dragons, back in <Time>".
    - *Streaks*: Tracks user contribution streaks -> Awards temporary "On Fire" status emoji.
